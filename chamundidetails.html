Shree Chamundeshwari Temple

Ticket Fare

Entry Free

There is no entry fee to visit Chamundi Hill or the Chamundeshwari Temple in Mysuru; entry is free for everyone. However,
if you want quicker darshan , you can opt for special or VIP queue tickets:

Special entry darshan ticket: ₹30–₹100 per person (for quicker darshan).
VIP darshan ticket: ₹100–₹300 per person (for fastest access).

The general entry—whether you go by road or climb the steps—is free, and there are no longer any vehicle entry fees to the hill.
