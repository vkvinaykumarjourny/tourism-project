WELCOME UDUPI DISTRICT

Udupi is a coastal district in the Indian state of Karnataka, known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful temples, and stunning beaches. The district is famous for its cuisine, particularly Udupi cuisine, which is vegetarian and has a unique blend of flavors. Udupi is also home to several educational institutions and is a hub for traditional Indian arts and crafts.
