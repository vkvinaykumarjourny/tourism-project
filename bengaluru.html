WELCOME TO BENGALURU DISTRICT

BENGALURU, Karnataka, is a popular tourist destination known for its historical temples, rich Hoysala architecture, and lush greenery. It's often called the "temple city" due to its numerous ancient temples, including the Hoysala temples at Mosale and the Lakshmi Narasimha Temple. Hassan also serves as a gateway to hill stations like Sakleshpur and Chikmagalur.
